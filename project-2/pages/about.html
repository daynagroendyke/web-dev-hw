Why this topic?

What is this life if it is spent living someone elses dream?
One of the most important choices we can make in life,
is the decision to embrace our uniqueness and share our gifts with the world.
The first step in that process is to define who we would like to be, and
then fully immerse ourselves in that quest of character discovery.
So, that we can become teachers, and contributors.
