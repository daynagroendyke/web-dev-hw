How to follow your path.

1. Do what you love.

Just do it.

Choose a job you love, and you will never work a day in your life.
-Confucius

2. Find inspiration, and create.

However it is necessary.

Find out what you love to do, and learn to do it.
Once you know what you love, find others who also enjoy doing that thing.
Next, get involved and become an expert.
Then you can share what you know, and teach others how to do what you do.
Repeat this, and help your commmunity grow.

However hard it may be.
